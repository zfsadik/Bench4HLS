resetn: active‑low synchronous reset; when low at a rising edge, the register is reset to 0
in: serial data input
out: output taken from the most‑significant bit (bit 3) of the shift register

The top-level function should have the following prototype:
void TopModule(bool resetn, bool in, bool &out)
