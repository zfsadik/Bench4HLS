  Present state y[2:0] | Next state y[2:0] x=0, Next state y[2:0] x=1 | Output z

  001 | 001, 100 | 0
  010 | 010, 001 | 0
  011 | 001, 010 | 1
  100 | 011, 100 | 1

The top-level function should have the following prototype:
void TopModule(bool reset, bool x, bool &z)
