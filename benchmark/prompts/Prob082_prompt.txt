Build a 4-bit shift register (right shift), with synchronous active-high reset, synchronous active high signals load, and enable.

  (1) reset: Resets shift register to zero.

  (2) load: Loads shift register with data[3:0] instead of shifting.

  (3) ena: Shift right (q[3] becomes zero, q[0] is shifted out and disappears).

  (4) q: The contents of the shift register. If both the load and ena inputs are asserted (1), the load input has higher priority.

The top-level function should have the following prototype:
void TopModule(bool reset, bool load, bool ena, ap_uint<4> data, ap_uint<4> &q)
