Assume you have two 8‑bit two’s complement numbers, a and b. These numbers are added to produce an 8‑bit result s, and you must also compute whether a signed overflow occurred.
The overflow is detected when the sign bits of a and b are the same but differ from the sign bit of s.

The top-level function should have the following prototype:
void TopModule(ap_int<8> a, ap_int<8> b, ap_int<8> &s, bool &overflow)
