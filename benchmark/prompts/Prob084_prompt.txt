Build a combinational circuit in HLS C++ with two 1‑bit inputs, a and b, and seven outputs defined as follows:

	1. out_and: a AND b
	2. out_or: a OR b
	3. out_xor: a XOR b
	4. out_nand: NOT (a AND b)
	5. out_nor: NOT (a OR b)
	6. out_xnor: a XNOR b
	7. out_anotb: a AND (NOT b)

The top-level function should have the following prototype:	
void TopModule(bool a, bool b,
               bool &out_and,
               bool &out_or,
               bool &out_xor,
               bool &out_nand,
               bool &out_nor,
               bool &out_xnor,
               bool &out_anotb)
