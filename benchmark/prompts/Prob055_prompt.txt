Implement a combinational circuit that, given four 1‑bit inputs (a, b, c, and d), produces a 1‑bit output (out) defined by the following Karnaugh map:

              ab
   cd   00  01  11  10
   00 | 1 | 1 | 0 | 1 |
   01 | 1 | 0 | 0 | 1 |
   11 | 0 | 1 | 1 | 1 |
   10 | 1 | 1 | 0 | 0 |

The top-level function should have the following prototype:   
void TopModule(bool a, bool b, bool c, bool d, bool &out)
