Build a combinational circuit with a 4‑bit input (in) that produces three outputs:

out_and: the AND reduction of the 4‑bit vector.
out_or: the OR reduction of the 4‑bit vector.
out_xor: the XOR reduction of the 4‑bit vector.

The top-level function should have the following prototype:
void TopModule(ap_uint<4> in, ap_uint<1> &out_and, ap_uint<1> &out_or, ap_uint<1> &out_xor)
